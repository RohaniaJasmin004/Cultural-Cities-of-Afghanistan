Cultural Cities of Afghanistan

Afghanistan is a country consisting of 34 provinces, and each province tells different stories, customs, and traditions.

Kandahar

Kandahar is one of the important cities of Afghanistan and a shining example of a history. Kandahar was known in the past as the gateway to Khorasan. Kandahar has many historical and tourist attractions.

Ghazni

Ghazni is famous for its star-shaped minarets from the 12th century. These minarets are the remains of the Bahram Shah Mosque. The surrounding areas of these minarets are decorated with geometric designs and verses from the Quran in Kufic script..
